A note on browser compatibility

My personal projects tend to be of a more experimental nature, so not all of them will work across all browsers. However, for public-facing client projects, I carefully tweak and test every site for compatibility with all major browsers, including two or three major versions of Internet Explorer. Each project on this page specifies the browsers with which they are compatible.
